About

Aux Battle, a game commonly played with no proper platform or rules. A game in which friends & family can battle and see who has the better music taste while being given certain prompts to answer. Using the Node.js Framework we were able to create this website which will make your playing easy and simple with pre-made prompts and bracket! Playing this game is a great way to bring the competitive spirt and have a good time.
